About

Solo piano vocalist with several years of international stage experience, Lara Bruyelles built her craft on the stages of major international cruise lines, performing for audiences of over 600 people in a theatre at sea.

She now performs in regular residency in Lyon, at the Sofitel Lyon Bellecour and the Mademoiselle Simone hotel, and has also worked as a keyboardist and backing vocalist for a London-based band, notably for weddings.

A repertoire of more than 500 songs, piano and voice, a natural stage presence and a real feel for the room, from intimate performance to interactive entertainment.
